Top Matches

India vs Pakistan - 2007 T20 World Cup Final

This thrilling encounter in 2007 saw India winning the inaugural ICC T20 World Cup by defeating arch-rivals Pakistan in a nail-biting finish. The moment MS Dhoni handed the ball to Joginder Sharma for the final over became iconic in Indian cricket history.

India vs Sri Lanka - 2011 World Cup Final

India's historic win in the 2011 World Cup final, held at the Wankhede Stadium, Mumbai, was a moment of great pride for the nation. MS Dhoni's match-winning six is still fresh in every Indian cricket fan's memory.

Sachin Tendulkar's 200th Test Match - 2013

One of the most emotional moments in cricket history, Sachin Tendulkar's farewell Test at Wankhede Stadium in 2013 saw the Master Blaster bid adieu to international cricket after an illustrious career.

India vs Australia - 2020-21 Test Series (Gabba Win)

India’s remarkable win at the Gabba during the 2020-21 series was one of the greatest Test matches in history. The victory helped India clinch the series in Australia, breaking Australia's 32-year unbeaten record at the Gabba.
